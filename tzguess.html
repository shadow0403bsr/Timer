What is this?

It is a tool which guesses the time zone from latitude and longitude.

How is it helpful?

It tells you when the Community Day will end at that place. A shiny doesn't remain shiny after the Community Day ends. So if your cool down is not going to end before the Community Day ends at that place, you'll lose the shiny.
